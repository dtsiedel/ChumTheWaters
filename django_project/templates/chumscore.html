About Chum Score

Our Chum Score is a measure of a summoner's success with a given champion. A value between 0 and 100, it indicates how much of a threat that player will be with that particular champion. The Chum Score is based on their winrate with the champion and a weighted Kill/Death/Assist score. A Chum Score requires at least five games with that champion to have any accuracy, and only counts ranked games. We hope the Chum Score allows you to easily assess threats on the enemy team at the beginning of a match.

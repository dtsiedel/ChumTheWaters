Our Philosophy

League of Legends is a game of skill. If you practice and learn from your mistakes, you will climb the ladder. However, sometimes it helps to have a leg up on the competition. This website cannot make you a more skilled player, but it can provide you with the knowledge to gain your competitive edge. Similar to how pros spend time analyzing their opponents, Chum the Waters aims to inform you of the basic playstyles of your opponents. Whether they like to spend time farming, or would rather play aggressively, Chum the Waters provides invaluable knowledge so that you can know your opponent before actually facing them in a match. This site was created by League of Legends players, in hopes that it will improve your solo queue experience. Happy climbing!
